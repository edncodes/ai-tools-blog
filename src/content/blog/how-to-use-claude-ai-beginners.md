Claude AI is one of the most capable free AI tools available right now. Here's how to get started in under 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Create a free account

Go to claude.ai and sign up with your email. The free plan gives you access to Claude Sonnet — which is more than enough to start.

## Step 2: Start your first conversation

Type a clear, specific question or task. The more detail you give Claude, the better the response.

## Step 3: Use it for real tasks

Start with things like summarizing articles, drafting emails, explaining concepts, or writing simple code.

## Step 4: Refine with follow-ups

Claude remembers your conversation. If the first response isn't right, just tell it what to change — it will improve.
