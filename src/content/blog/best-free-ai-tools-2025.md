AI tools have exploded in 2025, but most beginners don't know where to start. Here are the five I actually use and recommend.

## 1. Claude AI
Claude is one of the best free AI assistants available. You can use it to write, summarize, code, and research — all for free.

## 2. Notion AI
Notion now has a built-in AI that helps you write notes, summaries, and project plans inside your workspace.

## 3. Canva AI
Canva's AI features let you generate images, write copy, and design graphics without any design experience.

## 4. Perplexity AI
Think of it as a search engine powered by AI. It gives you direct answers with sources — perfect for research.

## 5. Google Gemini
Google's free AI assistant is deeply integrated with Gmail, Docs, and Drive — making it ideal if you are already in the Google ecosystem.
